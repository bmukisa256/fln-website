ABOUT US

The Female Lawyers’ Network (FLN) is a not-for-profit membership-based women in law network established in 2022. The FLN offers training and motivational sessions, empowerment sessions, mentorship and legal referral services with the aim to mentor and inspire women in law and girls. The FLN Membership cuts across sectors and age groups and encompasses members from the judiciary, private legal practice, in-house counsel, diplomatic mission, security forces, public service and law students. Further, the FLN membership comprises associate members who include the male Lawyers, male law students and any other people who embrace the FLN Agenda.
